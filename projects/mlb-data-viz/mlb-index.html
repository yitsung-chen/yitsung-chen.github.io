Glossary
* Notes :
Seasonal Average Stats : Transform career stats linearly to the scale of total games per season, estimated 162 games started for batters, and overall 68 games ( pitched + started) for pitchers.

Monthly Average Stats : Transform career stats linearly to the scale of total games per month ( 6 months in average per season ), estimated 27 games started for batters, and overall 11.23 games ( pitched + started) for pitchers.

Weekly Average Stats : Transform career stats linearly to the scale of total games per week ( 4 weeks in average per month ), estimated 6.25 games started for batters, and overall 2.83 games ( pitched + started) for pitchers.

( Please refer to BASEBALL REFERENCE for more details.)

    Metrics:
  • W : Total wins
  • SV : Total saves
  • HLD : Total holds
  • SO : Total strikeouts generated by pitcher
  • AO : Total fly outs
  • GO : Total ground outs
  • BB : Total base on balls
  • ERA : Average earned runs per 9-inning pitches
  • WHIP : ( Total BB + Total Hits ) / Total Pitched Innings
  • WPCT :
    Winning percentage = Total Wins / ( Total Wins + Total Losses )
  • K% : Total Strikeouts per Total Batters Faced
    Metrics:
  • H : Total hits (Including all bases hits & Home runs)
  • 1B : Total first-base hits
  • 2B : Total second-base hits
  • 3B : Total third-base hits
  • HR : Total home runs
  • RBI : Total Runs generated by batter's play
  • SB : Total Stolen bases
  • K : Total called strikeouts
  • AVG : Batting average = Total H / Total AB ( At Bats )
  • SLG : Slugging percentage, a batting productivity measurement
    = ( 1B + 2*2B + 3*3B + 4*HR ) / Total AB ( At Bats )
  • OBP : On-base percentage, measuring how frequently a batter reaches base.
    = ( Total H + Total BB + Total Hit by Pitch ) / ( Total AB ( At Bats ) + Total BB + Total Hit by Pitch + Total Sacrifice Fly )
  • OPS : On-base plus slugging, measuring the ability of a player both to get on base and to hit for power.
    = OBP + SLG
